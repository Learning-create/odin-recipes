Honey Roasted Chicken

Juicy, and crispy, what else is to say?

    Ingredients list:
  • 1 chicken, around 1,5 kg (3 lbs)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • a good pinch of pepper
  • 1/2 tsp dried tarragon
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 2 tbps melted butter
  • 1 tbsp honey
    Steps:
  1. Rub the chicken with salt and pepper and place it in a roasting pan.
  2. Mix honey and butter and brush the chicken with the mixture.
  3. Place in an oven preheated to 175 C (360 F) and roast until the chicken is tender and juices run clear (1 1/4 - 1 1/2 hours).
  4. Serve with broad beans.
