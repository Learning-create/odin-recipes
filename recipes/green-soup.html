    Ingredients list:
  • 3-1/2 to 5 oz. of fresh, parboiled spinach, or about 8 oz. of frozen whole spinach
  • 10 cm of the white part of a leek
  • 1 quart good bouillon (chopped finely cup 2)
  • Dash of pepper
  • Dash of ground ginger
  • 2 to 3 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Grated nutmeg
    Steps:
  1. Clean and rinse the fresh spinach or thaw the frozen
  2. Rinse the leek and slice thinly
  3. Bring the bouillon to a boil and add the spinach and leek. Let it boil for 5 minutes
  4. Add the parsley and boil together a few more minutes
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and ginger
  6. Whisk the yolks with the cream in the bottom of a soup tureen
  7. Pour in the soup while whisking briskly. Grate some nutmeg over the soup and serve it with a good bread.
