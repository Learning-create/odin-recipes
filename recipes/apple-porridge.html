Apple Porridge

Apple porridge

The best thing about porridge is the versatility, and the Norse knew that

    Ingredients list:
  • 3-4 dl coarse barley flour
  • 1 litre water
  • 2 apples and maybe a handful of hazelnuts
  • Salt
    Steps:
  1. Whisk down the flour a little at a time over boiling water.
  2. Continue boiling for 5 minutes while continuously stirring.
  3. The apples and chopped nuts are boiled the last minutes.
  4. Take the pot off the fire and place it close to the fire for 30 minutes to an hour.
  5. Slice the apples into small pieces. Boil them with a bit of water and add honey for flavouring. Adding stinging nettle seeds adds taste.

    6. The water can partially or entirely be replaced with milk, but then the porridge burns more easily. If you have made butter the butter milk from that can be used.
